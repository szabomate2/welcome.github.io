About Me

Hi there! I’m a dedicated DevOps engineer with a passion for optimizing and automating workflows to ensure seamless software delivery. One of my proudest achievements is building my own Kubernetes cluster for my homelab, which has significantly enhanced my hands-on experience and understanding of container orchestration. When I’m not immersed in the world of DevOps, I spend my free time diving into full-stack web development, constantly expanding my skill set and exploring new technologies. Outside of the tech realm, I have a creative side that I express through making leather goods. Crafting unique, high-quality leather items is not just a hobby for me, but a way to unwind and bring my artistic visions to life.
